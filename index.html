History

Equa is a little child with 3 years old, she doesn't sleep without her plush cat Nimity. In one raining night, the light is off, and Equa is hidden your self in the house, because can't find Nimity. Our hero need to find Equa and Nimity in the darkness.

Rules

The game consiste in a maze where you need to find two characters the fasted way posible Every player start with 100,000 of score, that score decrease a long the time, when one of the characters is founded the is score is increased the same value the player have in that moment. Press Start first to Player 1, when finished press start again to start Player 2 round, in the end the game shows who founded first Equa & Nimity
